Birthday Music File

To add background music to your birthday gift site:

1. Download a soft, gentle birthday music track (preferably instrumental)
2. Save it as "birthday-music.mp3" in this directory
3. Good sources for royalty-free birthday music:
   - YouTube Audio Library
   - FreeMusicArchive.org
   - Pixabay Music
   - Bensound.com
   - Zapsplat.com

Recommended search terms:
- "soft birthday music"
- "gentle piano birthday"
- "calm birthday instrumental"
- "happy birthday background music"

The audio will automatically play when the user reaches the gift page (after entering the special word).
